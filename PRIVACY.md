# Privacy Policy

This Discord bot is designed exclusively to manage and control a private Minecraft Bedrock server.

## Data Collection
This bot **does not collect, store, or share any personal data** from users.

The bot only reads message content in servers where it is installed for the sole purpose of:
- Recognizing commands
- Executing server-related actions

No messages are stored, logged permanently, or saved in any database.

## Data Usage
All data is processed in real time and discarded immediately after command execution.

## Data Sharing
No data is shared with third parties.

## Security
The bot operates locally and does not expose sensitive information.

## Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time without prior notice.
